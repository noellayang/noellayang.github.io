Quadruped Robot

Fusion 360 • ST3215-HS bus servos • URDF/MJCF export

Overview

Designed a 2-DoF-per-leg quadruped within a 300×300×300mm envelope. Built a parametric CAD assembly and exported to URDF/MJCF for simulation.

What I did

  • Mechanical design: hip/knee joints, double-shear mounting, compact torso.
  • System integration: compute, servo driver, battery packaging.
  • Simulation handoff: URDF/MJCF export for testing gaits virtually.
